Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Lorely is a mobile app that reveals the hidden history of places near you. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what we do not do with it. We've kept things simple because our approach to data is simple: collect only what's necessary, store as little as possible, and never sell or share your data.

Information We Collect

Location
Lorely requires access to your device's GPS location while the app is in use. Your coordinates are used to find historical stories within walking distance of where you are standing. Location is never stored on our servers linked to you — it is rounded to a neighbourhood-level precision (~1km) and used only as an anonymous cache key to serve stories to anyone nearby. We do not track your movements or build a history of places you have visited.

Story history
A list of stories you have opened is stored locally on your device so you can browse your history and see a daily featured story. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time from the Settings menu.

App usage counters
A daily story view count is stored locally on your device to manage the free tier limit. It resets automatically at midnight and is never sent to our servers.

Access codes
If you redeem a promotional access code, that code is stored locally on your device. It is checked against our database only at the time of redemption.

Bug reports
If you submit a bug report through the app, the text you write, your app version, and your device platform (iOS or Android) are sent to us by email. No location or personal details are included unless you choose to write them in your report.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account or email address is required to use Lorely
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • We do not track which stories you read or how long you spend in the app
  • We do not use advertising networks or share data with data brokers
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or photos

Third-Party Services

Lorely uses a small number of third-party services to function. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Supabase — Our backend database stores anonymously cached stories by location. No user accounts or identifiers are stored. supabase.com/privacy

RevenueCat — If you make an in-app purchase, RevenueCat processes the transaction and manages your entitlement status. They receive standard platform purchase data (device identifiers, purchase receipts) from Apple or Google. revenuecat.com/privacy

Sentry — If the app crashes, an error report is automatically sent including the crash details, app version, and device platform. No location or personal data is included. sentry.io/privacy

Wikipedia, Wikidata, OpenPlaques, Chronicling America, OpenStreetMap — Lorely queries these public historical databases using your approximate location to find nearby stories. These are free public services with no user accounts. Your location coordinates are sent as part of the search request.

Data Retention

Story history, usage counters, and access codes are stored only on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app. You can also clear your story history at any time from the Settings menu.

Anonymously cached stories on our servers are retained indefinitely but contain no personal information. Bug reports are retained in our email inbox until manually deleted.

Crash reports sent to Sentry are automatically deleted after 90 days.

Children's Privacy

Lorely is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
